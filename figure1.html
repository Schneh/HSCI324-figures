Each point represents a disease locus showing both strong pathogen-selection signals and elevated modern autoimmune risk. Data drawn from Fumagalli et al. (2011) and Quintana-Murci (2016). The positive trend reflects the hygiene hypothesis: alleles selected under high infectious burden now contribute to inflammatory disease in low-pathogen environments.
